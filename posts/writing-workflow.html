My Lightweight Writing Workflow

Every post starts as a note in one sentence. If I cannot summarize the point in one sentence, I am not ready to draft.

I keep a running list of 10 ideas, draft in 30-minute sessions, and ship imperfectly. Consistency beats brilliance when building a writing habit.
